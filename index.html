About me

I like to analyze data to answer research questions and test hypotheses. Currently I investigate questions related to the turbulence in the stable Atmospheric Boundary Layer through my work as a PhD in Physics student at Universidade Federal de Santa Maria in the Department of Physics.

I graduated as Physics teacher from the Universidad Distrital Francisco Jose de Caldas based in Bogotá, Colombia. Then I traveled to Brazil to start with a MS in Physics in 2016. In 2018 I began working on my PhD in Physics, where I am investigating statistical methods to parameterize the Atmospheric Boundary Layer under the advisement of Dr. Luca Mortarini at Italian National Research Council. I expect to graduate by the end of 2022.

I am a R enthusiast. I enjoy programming and photographing.
